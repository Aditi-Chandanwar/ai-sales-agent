# AI Sales Agent

## Project Overview

AI Sales Agent is an AI-powered lead generation and sales outreach system designed for **Genuine Automation Products LLP**, an industrial automation and sensor solutions company.

The objective is to automate repetitive sales activities such as identifying potential customers, extracting contact information, qualifying leads, and initiating the first outreach email while keeping sales engineers in control of business decisions.

---

# Business Problem

Sales teams spend significant time:

- Searching for potential customers
- Identifying whether companies are end users
- Finding contact information
- Sending introductory emails
- Tracking outreach manually

These repetitive activities reduce the time available for actual customer engagement and sales.

---

# Project Objective

Build an AI-assisted workflow that can:

- Discover potential industrial customers
- Filter only end-user companies
- Extract publicly available contact information
- Automatically send the first introduction email
- Maintain a lead database
- Prepare the workflow for future AI-assisted customer conversations

# Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

The first working version should:

1. Search for automotive companies in the selected region.
2. Identify end-user companies.
3. Extract:
   - Company Name
   - Website
   - Country
   - Public Email
   - Contact Person (if available)

4. Store qualified leads.
5. Automatically send the first introduction email using:
   [marketing@genuineautomation.com](mailto:marketing@genuineautomation.com)
6. Record the email status.

---

# Success Criteria

The MVP will be considered successful if it can:

- Generate 10 qualified leads
- Correctly identify end users
- Extract valid contact information
- Successfully send the first email
- Maintain a structured lead database

---

# Out of Scope (Current Version)

The following features are intentionally excluded:

- Automatic quotations
- Automatic pricing
- Autonomous negotiations
- Automatic technical recommendations
- CRM integration
- Follow-up automation

These features will be implemented in later versions.

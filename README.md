# AI Sales Agent

## Overview

AI Sales Agent is a project designed to help industrial sensor distributors automate parts of their sales workflow.

The project is inspired by a real-world business challenge faced by a sensor distribution company that wants to improve lead generation, customer outreach, and sales efficiency.

The goal is to explore how AI can assist humans in repetitive sales tasks while keeping people involved in important business decisions.

---

## Problem Statement

Industrial distributors often spend significant time on:

- Finding potential customers
- Collecting contact information
- Sending outreach emails
- Understanding customer requirements
- Matching requirements with suitable products

Many of these tasks are repetitive, manual, and time-consuming.

---

## Project Goal

Build an AI-assisted workflow that can:

- Discover potential leads
- Extract publicly available contact information
- Automate initial outreach
- Analyze customer responses
- Recommend relevant products
- Support human decision-making

---

## Development Roadmap

### Phase 1: Lead Discovery

- [ ] Find companies based on industry and region
- [ ] Collect company website information
- [ ] Extract publicly available contact details

### Phase 2: Outreach Automation

- [ ] Send predefined introduction emails
- [ ] Track outreach status

### Phase 3: Requirement Analysis

- [ ] Analyze customer responses using AI
- [ ] Extract requirements and intent

### Phase 4: Product Recommendation

- [ ] Match requirements with relevant products
- [ ] Generate draft responses for review
